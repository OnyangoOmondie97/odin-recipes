Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that layers pasta, cheese, and a meat or vegetable sauce. It is baked to achieve a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Over the years, various iterations of this dish have appeared worldwide, but the heart of lasagna remains the same: layers of deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna pasta sheets
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pound of ground beef or ground turkey
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil
  • ½ cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onions, sautéing until translucent.
  3. Add the ground beef or turkey to the pan. Cook until browned, breaking up the meat as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat and let the sauce simmer for about 20 minutes.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, half of the mozzarella cheese, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix until well combined.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom.
  7. Place 3 lasagna sheets over the sauce. Spread a portion of the cheese mixture over the pasta sheets, then top with a layer of the meat sauce. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  10. After 25 minutes, remove the foil and continue baking for another 10-15 minutes or until the cheese on top is golden brown and bubbly.
  11. Remove from the oven and let the lasagna sit for about 10 minutes before serving. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to cut and serve.
  12. Serve with a side of salad or garlic bread, and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
