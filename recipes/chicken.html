Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan, also known as Chicken Parmigiana, is a delicious combination of crispy breaded chicken, marinara sauce, and melted cheese. Perfectly paired with pasta or bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs. In a separate dish, mix breadcrumbs with parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken breast in the eggs, then coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet and fry each chicken piece until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  5. Place chicken pieces in a baking dish. Top each with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and basil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot, ideally with spaghetti or your favorite pasta.
